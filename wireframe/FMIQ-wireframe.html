Why mobile-first matters

Field staff don’t adopt a system they can’t use at the point of work. If the technician can’t use it by week 4, the data goes stale and the system becomes a reporting front-end nobody operates. This is the No.1 adoption risk — so the shell ships in Sprint 3–4, not Sprint 11.
